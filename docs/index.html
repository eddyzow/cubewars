WHAT IS THE TESSERACT?

The Tesseract is the online ranked mode of Cube Wars. Begin your journey at the base of the Tesseract, zero meters. Rise up the Tesseract when you win and fall down when you lose. Rise high enough and be promoted to higher ranks. Who will prove strong enough to stay at the top?
